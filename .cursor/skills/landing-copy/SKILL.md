# Landing copy (Majico)

Apply when writing marketing or waitlist copy for klaut.pro.

- German primary. Direct claims. No em/en dashes. No stock "X, not Y" antithesis.
- Hero: wordmark first, one headline, one lede, one CTA (waitlist). No stats or cards in the first viewport.
- Positioning: agentic AI service layer / workspace for agents (Mail, Secrets, Books, Search, Database, Storage, Literature).
- Tone: professional, authoritative (Sage + Ruler). Prefer commas, periods, parentheses.

Tokens and motion live in `app/globals.css` / DESIGN.md (Majico).
